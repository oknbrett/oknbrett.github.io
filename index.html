About

I'm Brett. Vietnamese-Dutch, based in the Netherlands. Reformed Christian, supply chain student, occasional thinker.

This blog is my attempt to write through what I'm reading in Scripture — slowly, carefully, honestly. I came to Reformed theology young, through Ephesians 1 and Romans 9, and I'm still working out what it means.

The name comes from Come Thou Fount. "Prone to wander, Lord I feel it." That's the honest starting point.
